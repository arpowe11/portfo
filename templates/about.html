About me

Hi, I’m Alexander — a driven technologist currently pursuing a Master’s in Software Engineering and a Graduate Certificate in Artificial Intelligence from Penn State University. I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Cybersecurity from the University of South Florida, and I’m passionate about building intelligent, secure, and scalable software systems. With a strong foundation in programming, web systems, and leadership, I’m on a mission to create impactful tech solutions that shape the future.

With 7 years of experience in web management and Python programming, I’ve developed a strong foundation in web systems. Through Zero to Mastery Academy, I’ve deepened my expertise in frameworks like Django and Flask. I’m a committed lifelong learner, quickly picking up new technologies as needed, and thrive in roles that challenge me to grow and adapt in new areas of tech.

Beyond tech, I am a proud BSA Eagle Scout, a member of the National Society of Leadership and Success, and a member of the Computing Professional, demonstrating my dedication to teamwork, leadership, and communication.
